About the Founder

Meet Emelino T. Maestro, CPA

Welcome to TaxSpecialista Resources. I'm Emelino T. Maestro, a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) and the founder of this service. I started this platform for one simple reason: to empower Filipino taxpayers with the knowledge and tools they need to navigate the complexities of the BIR with confidence.

With over 40 years of experience, my career has been dedicated to mastering the Philippine Tax Code. As a Tax Code Specialist and a champion of taxpayer's rights, I've seen firsthand how stressful tax compliance can be. This deep expertise allows me to create practical, effective solutions for your most pressing tax challenges.

My mission is to replace confusion with clarity. Whether through a detailed digital template or a one-on-one consultation, I am committed to providing you with the professional support you deserve.
